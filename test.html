Established in 1927, Black Brook was designed by Bertie Way – who also designed The Country Club of Detroit, the Mayfield County Club and the famous South Course at Firestone Country Club in Akron. Black Brook Golf Course & Practice Center has been owned and operated by the City of Mentor since 2005. The 18th hole was selected as one of News Channel 5’s Dream 18, the Plain Dealer’s Elite 18, and the course was voted one of the Best Places to Play by Golf Digest magazine. Black Brook Golf Course & Practice Center has been named the “Best of the Best” in the golf course category by readers of The News-Herald four times!
